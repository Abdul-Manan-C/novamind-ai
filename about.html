Our Journey

FROM IDEA
TO IMPACT

2022
FOUNDED IN A GARAGE

Three engineers — Elena, Marcus, and James — quit their Big Tech jobs and started building NovaMind in a San Francisco garage. Fueled by conviction that AI tooling was broken for 99% of companies.

2022
FIRST 100 CUSTOMERS

Launched private beta. Reached 100 paying customers in 6 weeks through developer communities. Zero marketing spend — pure word of mouth.

2023
$4M SEED ROUND

Closed oversubscribed seed round led by Horizon Ventures. Grew team to 15 engineers and launched the Automation Engine — our flagship product.

2023
SOC 2 CERTIFICATION

Achieved SOC 2 Type II certification and signed first enterprise contracts. Deployed NovaMind into Fortune 500 infrastructure for the first time.

2024
$24M SERIES A

Led by SequoiaAI Partners with participation from a16z and Insight Partners. Used to scale engineering to 52 people across 3 time zones and launch the Predictive Intelligence Engine.

2025
12,000 TEAMS. 18 COUNTRIES.

NovaMind now serves teams across finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and tech — processing over 2.4 billion API requests per month with 99.9% uptime.

Our Values

WHAT WE
STAND FOR

01 — Principle
RADICAL TRANSPARENCY

We show our work. Every model decision is explainable. Every pricing change is communicated in advance. No hidden fees, no black boxes.

02 — Principle
OBSESSIVE RELIABILITY

Downtime is a broken promise. We engineer for 99.99% uptime and treat every incident as a personal failure. Reliability is non-negotiable.

03 — Principle
DEMOCRATIZE AI

Enterprise-grade intelligence shouldn't require enterprise-grade budgets. We price to make NovaMind accessible to teams of 2 and teams of 2,000.

04 — Principle
PRIVACY FIRST

Your data is yours. We never train on your private data without consent. Full GDPR and CCPA compliance is the floor, not the ceiling.

05 — Principle
SHIP, THEN ITERATE

We deploy weekly. Imperfect and live beats perfect and delayed. We listen hard, move fast, and treat feedback as a gift.

06 — Principle
RESPONSIBLE AI

We maintain an AI ethics board and publish annual transparency reports. We believe powerful AI demands proportional accountability.
