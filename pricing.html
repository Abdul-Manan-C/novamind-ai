FAQ

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Is there a free trial?
Yes — every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated automatically.
What counts as an API request?
Every call to our inference, analytics, or automation API counts as one request. Dashboard views and internal health checks are not counted.
Do you offer nonprofit or academic discounts?
Yes! Nonprofits and academic institutions receive 40% off any plan. Contact our sales team with verification documents to get your discount applied.
Is my data secure and private?
NovaMind is SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never train models on your private data without explicit consent.
